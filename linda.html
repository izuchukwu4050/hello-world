Gas explosion at a restaurant in Abuja leaves 11 people badly injured. Today eleven people suffered varying degrees of burns from a gas explosion that occured at Rosy restaurant Area 11,Abuja. The victims who were immediately taken to Asokoro general hospital suffered some 40 to 50 degree burns. A pregnant woman who simply identify herself as Ajayi who works at the restaurant said they were all going going about there various busiesses when the gas suddenly exploded. Ajayi who is three months pregnant said she needed to go for scanning to ascertain the condition of her baby.

news agency of nigeria report that the incident affected her face, legs and hands. She was thereby attended to by the hospital management to if she will be transfered to a better hospital to be given a proper treatment. Dr Aminu Mile, the Acting Director, Health and Human services Secretary (HHSS), Federal Capital Territory Administration (FCTA) while on a visit to the victim said the circumstances surrounding will be unrivalled. Dr Nnabuchi Chidi, Acting Management Director, Asokoro Hospital said that the hospital would refer some of the victim to the national hospital. He said that 11 of them were brought to the hospital and hospital would do everything to ensure that they are stabilised.
